Our Services

At Nipon Fabrication, we offer a wide range of services to meet your home and commercial fabrication needs. Our expertise ensures quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

1. Roofing Solutions

We provide durable and aesthetically pleasing roofing solutions that protect your home from the elements while enhancing its appearance. Our services include:

  • Metal roofing installation
  • Roof repairs
  • Custom roofing designs

2. Custom Gates

Our custom gates are designed to provide security and elegance to your property. We specialize in:

  • Decorative wrought iron gates
  • Sliding and swing gates

3. Structural Fabrication

We offer structural fabrication services to ensure the strength and stability of your home or commercial building. Our services include:

  • Truss fabrication and installation
  • Steel frame structures
  • Reinforcement and repairs

4. Staircase Fabrication

Our custom staircases are crafted to blend functionality with modern design. We provide:

  • Spiral staircases
  • Metal stairs
  • Custom railing designs

5. Welding Services

Our welding services are performed by skilled professionals to ensure precision and durability. We specialize in:

  • Custom metal fabrication
  • Window frames
  • Railings
